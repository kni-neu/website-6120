George Ignatius is a Staff ML Engineer at DoorDash working on client side inventory understanding, using LLM Agents to facilitate restaurant onboarding. In his engineering tenure, he was an Engineering Manager and Staff Applied Scientist at Etsy, a MLE at Facebook / Meta, Amazon, and Yelp!. His interests have been in Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and the application and improvements of LLMs and Agents.
